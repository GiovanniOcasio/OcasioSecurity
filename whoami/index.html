Welcome to Ocasio Security. Our story begins with a commitment to service and excellence. I proudly served as a Joint Terminal Attack Controller (JTAC) in the United States Marine Corps, completing two deployments — one to Norway and another to the Persian Gulf. During my military service, I displayed dedication to duty and served honorably. Not only was I instilled with discipline, commitment, and resilience, but I instilled these same traits into my junior Marines.

After transitioning from the military, I applied my dedication and expertise to the field of cybersecurity. Today, I work as a Cybersecurity Engineer, supporting USAF systems and ensuring the security of critical infrastructure.
